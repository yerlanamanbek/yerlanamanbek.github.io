Students from **Nazarbayev University (NU)** have achieved notable success at the **Siberian Mathematical Olympiad**, an internationally recognized mathematics competition organized by **Novosibirsk State University**  
([https://smc.nsu.ru/en](https://smc.nsu.ru/en){:target="_blank"}).

The **NU Math Team** was awarded a **Second Degree Diploma**, demonstrating a high level of mathematical proficiency and strong performance in a competitive international environment.

In addition to the team award, NU students received the following individual distinctions:

- **Second Degree Diploma**:  
  Gusseritsev Yevgeniy, Kairolla Madiyar, Above Abuzer
- **Third Degree Diploma**:  
  Zhaikul Turar

These results highlight the strong academic preparation and problem-solving skills fostered at Nazarbayev University. The University congratulates the students on 
their achievements and commends them for successfully representing NU at a prestigious international mathematics competition.

###### Photo: NU and Kazakhstan MSU students and Bakhytbek Zhumanov (Team Leader) at IMC in Bulgaria, 2019.

The IMC is one of prestigious competitions for students, organized with the joint participation of the American University in Bulgaria and University College London.
Over the past twenty-five competitions students from more than 200 institutions from over 65 countries all over the world have participated. 
Problems given for students are from the fields of Algebra, Real and Complex Analysis, Geometry and Combinatorics. 

We wish to acknowledge the effort of students and the assistance of Prof. Adilet Otemissov and Prof. Yerlan Amanbek.
