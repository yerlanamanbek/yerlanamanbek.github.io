Students from **Nazarbayev University (NU)** have achieved notable success at the **Siberian Mathematical Olympiad**, an internationally recognized mathematics competition organized by **Novosibirsk State University** ([https://smc.nsu.ru/en](https://smc.nsu.ru/en){:target="_blank"}).

The **NU Math Team** was awarded a **Second Degree Diploma**, demonstrating a high level of mathematical proficiency and strong performance in a competitive international environment.

In addition to the team award, NU students received the following individual distinctions:

- **Second Degree Diploma**:  
  Gusseritsev Yevgeniy, Kairolla Madiyar, Above Abuzer
- **Third Degree Diploma**:  
  Zhaikul Turar

These results highlight the strong academic preparation and problem-solving skills fostered at Nazarbayev University. The University congratulates the students on 
their achievements and commends them for successfully representing NU at a prestigious international mathematics competition.
