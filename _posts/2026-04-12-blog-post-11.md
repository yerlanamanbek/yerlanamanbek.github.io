The Republican Mathematical Olympiad 2026 among university students was successfully held at Nazarbayev University in Astana **from April 11 to April 12**,, 
bringing together 44 students from 9 universities across the country. The event served as a dynamic platform for talented young mathematicians 
to demonstrate their problem-solving abilities, exchange ideas, and engage in high-level academic competition.

The organizers express their sincere gratitude to all participants, team leaders, and jury members for contributing to the success of the event. 
The Olympiad continues to strengthen the mathematical community and inspire future generations of scholars.

Based on the **team results**:

-  **First place** — [Nazarbayev University](https://nu.edu.kz/){:target="_blank"} (**79.5 points**)
-  **Second place** — [Kazakh-British Technical University](https://kbtu.edu.kz/ru/){:target="_blank"} (**75.5 points**)
-  **Third place** — [SDU University](https://sdu.edu.kz/){:target="_blank"} (**54 points**)




Students from **Nazarbayev University (NU)** have achieved notable success at the **Siberian Mathematical Olympiad**, an internationally recognized mathematics competition organized by **Novosibirsk State University** ([https://smc.nsu.ru/en](https://smc.nsu.ru/en){:target="_blank"}).

The **NU Math Team** was awarded a **Second Degree Diploma**, demonstrating a high level of mathematical proficiency and strong performance in a competitive international environment.

In addition to the team award, NU students received the following individual distinctions:

- **Second Degree Diploma**:  
  Gusseritsev Yevgeniy, Kairolla Madiyar, Above Abuzer
- **Third Degree Diploma**:  
  Zhaikul Turar

These results highlight the strong academic preparation and problem-solving skills fostered at Nazarbayev University. NU congratulates the students on 
their achievements and commends them for successfully representing NU at a prestigious international mathematics competition.
